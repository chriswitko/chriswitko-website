Full Stack Developer

Chris Witko

Hi. My name is Chris Witko. I live in London. I’m a Full Stack Developer. For the last two years, I worked for Inskin Media Ltd in London as a Senior Developer.
I started my career almost 20 years ago. Last two years I worked as a team member of 6 developers. I'm responsible for support and develop internal applications, but also build a new one, and actively support the whole team.
On my everyday basis, I love to build up my skills in programming. Recently I "fall in love" with testing frameworks (Jest is freaking super), Styled-Components and React are my everyday stack. I always complain about a lack of time, but part of my spare time I spend on side-projects, which are the best way to learn. Recently, I’ve released my two side projects (https://thepressreview.com) where I used React, Redux, Sagas, and ApiLab (https://github.com/chriswitko/apilab) to upgrade my skills with Node, Mongo and testing.
I’m currently learning more about Machine Learning using simple algorithms to build recommendations engines, and also learn to use TensorFlow.
I hope that this short intro helps you to learn a little bit about me. I'm open to discussion and learn about your company, team and projects.

Offers

01. Digital products as an unwavering entanglement between copy, art direction, design, and coding

Web applications are complex entangled layered systems; the design of your database will have a direct impact on the interface design as well as the responsiveness of a website which may lead to a typography choice. It is why we promote a fluid horizontal workflow wherein each individual has a global vision of the process. From semiotic to wireframing, branding to prototyping, custom typography to front-end engineering, we guarantee the delivery of the most engaging experiences.

02. To provide sustainable and algorithm-proof strategies for long-term results

In order to find the optimal solution, built to scale, performance, and longevity we are constantly testing and evolving the interface with iterative processes, in real time. Our capabilities include responsive development, web and native applications, commerce, front-end engineering, testing, technical consulting, pattern library development.

03. Rich content & Copywriting are key investments

Every brand must think in terms of a publisher in order to develop a unique and compelling strategy with regards to content. Not limited to text but more widely to any online material, graphic images, reviews, blogs, sponsorship etc. Creating the content, localisation, delivery, and governance will define the whole editorial content development process. These groups of signs need to be organized by each entity , their (ies,) attributes, and interrelationships, referring to the database, clean outlines and experimental search engines optimize (ation) patterns such as structured data and semantic interoperability. Our range of capabilities include copywriting, translation / localisation, photography, video and editing.

05. Extract relevant direction from the data analysis

Analytics tools will help visualize customer behaviour in real-time whilst analysis processes will guide you in taking the right actions. We build graphic representations of these processes to achieve results that help the understanding and communication.

06. Social media as a meta-discourse

Social media has changed the way people live. Online or off we resort to social media for almost all aspects in our daily lives. and turned any stories in something participatory. Aside from the main branded discourse taking place on channels such as Twitter or Facebook, you can listen and interact with the most engaged people in your audience, get feedback about how they sense, talk and share your image.

07. Off-site and collateral materials

Aside from our digital practice, we have extensive experience in traditional media and possess a true passion for typography, books and all print material and thrive to reach a balance between print and digital media.
